# Flat Rock Technology — Employee Onboarding Policy

Every new hire must have a full name, work email, department, reporting manager, and start date before onboarding can be approved.
The reporting manager must belong to the same department as the new hire.
Work email addresses must use the @flatrock.com domain.
Start dates must be a valid future date; onboarding cannot be backdated.
Contract employees must have a defined contract duration in months.
Salary must be confirmed by Finance before approval; a salary marked "to be confirmed" requires human review.
Any onboarding document with missing required fields must be sent for human review.
